Commands

Here you will find commands for Haseul Bot and instructions on how to use them.

  • The default prefix for commands is .
  • Hover over or long press commands to see their aliases
  • Hover over or long press arguments to see examples
  • Required arguments are in [brackets]
  • Optional arguments are in (parentheses)
  • Required permissions are in PURPLE
